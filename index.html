OneLens

Use OneLens as your one app to upload photos directly to your cloud services and keep work/educational photos separate from your personal memories.

  • Download on the App Store

Effortless Uploads

Take photos and videos and let OneLens upload them directly to your setup cloud services.
You can set up multipel multipel colud services, and OneLens will automatically upload to all at once. The camera is allways ready, no need to set filename or destinan every time - you take the photo - OneLens uploads.

Using OneLens you photos will always be separate from your iPhone's cameraroll, ideal for work photos.

Avalibel services:

OneDrive

Google Drive

With more comming

De-clutter Work and Personal Photos

Photos taken with OneLens are uploaded directly to the cloud. So that you can easily declutter and seperate for example work and personal photos and videos. OneLens maks it easy to:

*Use iPhone Camera app for personal photos -> photos app on phone

*Use OneLens for work photos -> Onedrive/GoogleDrive

Launch, Capture and Share

With OneLens setup on your lock screen you can capture and share a picture from your online OneLens Camera Roll with-in secounds.

OneDrive and Google Drive have their own folder viewer. In the OneLens camera roll you can

*Share: Attach to mail, Copy, Message etc

*View photo details: Size, focal length, taken date

*Delete the image form the cloud

All without needing to open other apps

Destination Folder and Format Control

You choose what folder on your cloud service the images are saved to, and the naming structure. You are also in control of formats of your photos, you can choose between:

*JPEG

*PNG

*HEIC

Security and Privacy
