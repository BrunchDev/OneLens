Photo app for direct-to-cloud upload


OneLens

OneLens is a camera app built take photos and upload photos directly to your cloud services and keep work/school photos separate from your personal memories.

  • Download on the App Store

Effortless Uploads

Take photos and videos and let OneLens upload them automatically to one or more cloud services.

No filenames to enter. No folders to choose. Just open the camera, capture, and move on.

Available cloud services

  • OneDrive
  • Google Drive
  • More coming soon

De-clutter Work and Personal Photos

Photos taken with OneLens are uploaded directly to the cloud. So that you can easily declutter and seperate for example work and personal photos and videos. OneLens maks it easy to:

*Use iPhone Camera app for personal photos -> photos app on phone

*Use OneLens for work photos -> Onedrive/GoogleDrive

Launch, Capture, and Share — in Seconds

With OneLens placed on your Lock Screen or Action Button, you can launch the app, take photos, and share them within seconds.

  • Share via Mail, Messages, AirDrop, and more
  • View photo details such as date, size, and focal length
  • Delete files directly from the cloud

Full Control Over Files and Formats

Choose exactly where and how your photos are stored.

Define destination folders, filename structures, and image formats to match your professional workflow.

  • JPEG
  • PNG
  • HEIC

Security and Privacy

We store no personal data and use OAuth2 and trusted APIs from Microsoft and Google to ensure your privacy.
