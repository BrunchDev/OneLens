Photo app for direct-to-cloud upload


OneLens

OneLens is a camera app built take photos and upload photos directly to your cloud services and keep work/school photos separate from your personal memories.

  • Download on the App Store

Effortless Uploads

Take photos and videos and let OneLens upload them automatically to one or more cloud services.

No filenames to enter, no folders to select, and no repeated prompts. You open the camera, take the photo, and OneLens uploads it.

Photos taken with OneLens are always kept separate from your iPhone’s camera roll, making it ideal for work and study use.

Available cloud services:
• OneDrive
• Google Drive
• More coming soon

De-clutter Work and Personal Photos

Keep work and personal photos completely separate.

Photos taken with OneLens are uploaded directly to the cloud and never appear in your iPhone’s Photos app.

This makes it easy to maintain a clean personal camera roll while keeping all work, school, or project photos organised and secure.

Typical usage:
• iPhone Camera app → Personal photos in Photos app
• OneLens → Work photos in OneDrive or Google Drive

Launch, Capture, and Share

With OneLens placed on your Lock Screen or Action Button, you can launch the app, capture photos, and share them within seconds.

There is no need to choose filenames, folders, or upload destinations while working.

From the built-in OneLens camera roll you can:
• Share photos via Mail, Messages, AirDrop, and more
• View photo details such as date, size, and focal length
• Delete photos directly from the cloud

All actions are available without opening any other apps.

Destination Folder and Format Control

You decide where and how your photos are stored in the cloud.

OneLens lets you choose the destination folder, filename structure, and image format to match professional workflows.

Supported image formats:
• JPEG
• PNG
• HEIC

Security and Privacy

We store no personal data and use OAuth2 and trusted APIs from Microsoft and Google to ensure your privacy.
