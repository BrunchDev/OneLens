Privacy Policy

Welcome to OneLens! We prioritize your privacy and trust in our app. Here's how we handle your information:

Your Personal Information

We're committed to keeping your personal information confidential. Our app thrives because of happy users who pay to use it – no ads, and no selling of your data, ever.

Local Photos/Videos

In between capture and upload, a temporary video or photo will be stored on your phone. Once safely in your cloud, it will be deleted. If you save photos or videos on your iPhone using OneLens, they're entirely under your control. We can't view it or assist in recovering lost data.

Cloud Uploads

Should you choose to upload photos or videos to a cloud service, please note that we don't have access to that data. Be sure to review the privacy policy of the specific cloud service you're using.
